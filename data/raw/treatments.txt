Dialysis Treatments Overview

1. **Starting Dialysis**  
    Preparing for dialysis can be a challenging transition. Understanding what to expect can help you adapt to your new treatment routine with greater ease.

2. **Home Hemodialysis (HHD)**  
    Home hemodialysis is a form of hemodialysis performed in the comfort of a patient's home, offering flexibility and convenience.

3. **Peritoneal Dialysis (PD)**  
    Peritoneal dialysis is currently the most prevalent home-based dialysis treatment option, providing patients with an effective and accessible modality.

4. **Staff-Assisted Home Hemodialysis (SAHHD)**  
    The Staff-Assisted Home Hemodialysis program, offered by Dialysis Care Centers, is designed to provide patient-centered care and support for home-based treatments.

5. **In-Center Hemodialysis**  
    In-center hemodialysis is one of several treatment options available, allowing patients to receive care in a clinical setting under professional supervision.
